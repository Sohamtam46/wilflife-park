ABOUT US

Amuay Wildlife Park is a pristine nature reserve located 350 km west of Venezuela's capital city of Caracas. Spanning over 72 square kilometers, the park is home to an extraordinary diversity of wildlife and plant species.

PRESERVING NATURE

Committed to conservation and ecological sustainability, the Venezuelan government strictly limits vehicle access to protect the natural landscape and ensure the safety of both visitors and wildlife. Scheduled guided tours along designated dirt tracks allow visitors an immersive experience while preserving the delicate ecosystem. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to protect the natural landscape, ensuring safe habitats for numerous species and promoting sustainable interactions with nature. Through guided tours, educational programs, and community engagement, we strive to inspire visitors of all ages to appreciate, respect, and conserve the incredible biodiversity of our region.
